The Evolution of Roblox

Discover how Roblox grew from a small experiment to a global gaming phenomenon.

From a simple game platform in 2004 to a metaverse in 2025 – explore the full journey of Roblox!

🔹 2004 – The Beginning: DynaBlocks

David Baszucki and Erik Cassel created the project "DynaBlocks," later renamed "Roblox."

🏗 2006 – Official Roblox Launch

Roblox officially launched, featuring multiplayer, avatars, and the in-game currency Robux.

🎭 2007 – 2012: Early Growth

Roblox introduced the Roblox Studio, Builders Club, achievements, and new physics.

🌎 2013 – 2016: Global Expansion

Improved lighting, mobile support, and a new logo helped Roblox expand worldwide.

🚀 2017 – 2019: Explosive Popularity

Roblox reached 50 million users, launched on Xbox, and introduced a new physics engine.

🌌 2020 – 2023: The Metaverse Era

Roblox reached 150M+ monthly players, launched an IPO, and introduced AI-powered NPCs.

🎮 2024 – 2025: The Future of Roblox

With AI, AR, and new developer tools, Roblox continues to evolve.
